WHO IS AN ADVENTURER (ISFP)?
An Adventurer (ISFP) is a person with the Introverted, Observant, Feeling,
and Prospecting personality traits.
They tend to have open minds, approaching life, new experiences,
and people with grounded warmth.
Their ability to stay in the moment helps them uncover exciting potentials.
